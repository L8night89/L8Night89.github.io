About

L8 Night89 started off as a passion project that resulted in me having to sell all of my belongings (music equipment included) minus one guitar and move all the way around to the other side of the world in Singapore. Since Singapore has a thriving AI community I decided to take what I know (very little) about creating music and writing songs and create L8 Night89. I write songs and create music with the assist of AI creation tools like Suno also while blending various tools and instruments I have lying around to help create and guide the melody in the direction I want it to go. If you made it this far in my story you are appreciated and thanks for your support.
